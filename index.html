Welcome to London, Ontario

Day 1: City Overview

Location: London, Ontario (42.9849° N, 81.2453° W)
Nearby areas: Bayfield, Chatham-Kent, Goderich
Time Zone: Eastern Standard Time (EST)
Population: 410,003 (1.03% of Canada's population)

Day 2: Climate

Climate: Humid continental
Average Annual Temperature: Around 8°C (46°F)
Total Annual Precipitation: Approximately 1,000 mm (39 inches)
Annual Temperature Range: From sub-zero in winter to highs averaging around 25°C (77°F) in summer
Factors: Influenced by latitude, prevailing winds, elevation, proximity to Lake Huron, and the Great Lakes system (LOWERN)

Day 3: Landform & Vegetation

Topographic features: Lowland area with the Thames River, farmland, forests, gentle elevations
Vegetation: Mixed wood forests, suitable for various types of vegetation

Day 6: Resources & Industries

Resources: Agricultural products (grains, livestock), freshwater from the Thames River

Employment opportunities: Diverse range across various sectors

Day 7: Immigration

Reasons for immigration to Canada: Job opportunities, higher quality of life, education, better medical care, security, diverse society
London, Ontario's offerings: Real estate opportunities, quality education, diverse educational paths, good medical facilities, shopping, entertainment
