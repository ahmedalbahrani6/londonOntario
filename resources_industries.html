Resources & Industries - London, Ontario

Day 6: Resources & Industries

The city takes pride in its fields of education, healthcare, and manufacturing. All of these industries contribute significantly to the region because of their economic contributions. Because there are so many fertile agricultural lands, managing agriculture and its resources is simple.

Resouces:

  • Agricultural Resources: The region has fertile land making farming easy for various crops like corn, soybeans, and wheat. Agriculture is a significant resource in london.
  • Water Resources: London benefits from the Thames River, giving them a water resource for various purposes like agriculture, industrial use, and municipal water supply.
  • Human Resources: London is known for its educated workforce with schools like the University of Western Ontario contributing to a skilled group of workers. Skilled workers in healthcare, technology, education, and manufacturing sectors are very good resources in the city.

Industries:

  • Healthcare: London is well known for its hospitals, medical research facilities and clinics which cater to the region's healthcare needs.
  • Manufacturing: London has diverse manufacturing centers which include automotive, aerospace and food processing industries.
  • Education: London's educational institutions contribute significantly to the economy because of its universities and colleges which offer employment innovation.

Employment Opportunities:

  • Healthcare (tertiary): Opportunities in nursing, medical research and administrative jobs within hospitals and healthcare facilities.
  • Manufacturing & Industrial (all three sectors are available): Employment in automotive manufacturing, food processing and aerospace industries.
  • Education & Research (tertiary): Teaching positions, administrative roles and research opportunities within educational institutions and research centers.
